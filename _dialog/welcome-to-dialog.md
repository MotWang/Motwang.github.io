## Why a separate space

The research section on this site is where projects go once they've been through enough drafts to feel defensible. Dialog is the opposite: a place for shorter posts that don't need to be tied off yet — reading notes, one-off arguments, half-formed frameworks.

## What lands here

- Reading digests from arXiv, industry decks, or long-form pieces
- Working notes on ideas I'm still pressure-testing
- Reflections on the interplay between AI, SaaS, and capital markets

## Cadence

No cadence — new posts appear when there's something worth writing down. Filter chips at the top of the section will grow as more topics accumulate.
