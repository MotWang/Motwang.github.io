## Overview

Research initiative supporting Tencent's **AI Agent market entry decisions**, comparing industry and functional distributions of enterprise AI agent deployments globally and in China.

## Methodology

- Pivot table analysis of Salesforce and Chinese ToB AI Agent cases
- Industry/functional distribution mapping since 2025
- Specialized domestic scenario identification

## Key Findings

Chinese ToB AI Agent adoption concentrates in vertical-specific workflow automation, creating differentiated opportunities for domestic model providers and cloud vendors vs. horizontal platform plays.
