## Overview

Strategic consulting project supporting **Super App mini-program development** for a leading European telecom operator, referencing global successful Super App cases across Africa and Middle East markets.

## Deliverables

- Scenario selection and growth strategy design
- Operation and commercialization model frameworks
- GMV and user penetration models (bottom-up & top-down)

## Scenarios Covered

Games, short dramas, e-commerce, and 6 additional verticals with diversified monetization (advertising, payment, etc.).

## Impact

Provided actionable roadmap for client global resource deployment and Africa/Middle East market entry via mini-program ecosystem.
